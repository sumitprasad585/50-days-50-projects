About Feliks Zemdegs

Feliks Aleksanders Zemdegs is an Australian Rubik's Cube speedsolver. He is the only speedcuber ever to win the World Cube Association World Championship twice, winning in 2013 and 2015,and has set more than 100 world records across various speedcubing events.He is often considered the greatest speedsolver of all time.

About Kevin Hays

Kevin Hays (born 12 May 1994) is an American Rubik's Cube speedcuber. Recognized as an expert at solving big cubes (5x5, 6x6 and 7x7), he has won 6 world championship titles and set 21 world records across the three events. He has also set 47 North American records and won 21 US National titles.
